Potential Center to support the growth of Dhamma in the Bay Area

Over the past year, the search committee has evaluated over 30 properties with the goal
of finding a property for developing a Vipassana meditation center close to SF Bay Area.
In May 2017, the committee found a potential property near Watsonville in Monterey County.

Centrally located in California 
close to San Jose

The potential property is located off of Vega Road in a rural area near Watsonville, the property is 54 miles from San Jose airport and 20 miles from Santa Cruz.

PHASE 1
The 106 acres property that we are considering is valued at $1.6M by the seller. We have submitted a Letter Of Intent to purchase the property for $1.5M. Additionally, we would need about $5M for construction.

If we are able to purchase this property the plan is to start development for 60 to 80 students sometime in 2018.

PHASE 2
The goal would be to expand the capacity of the center to
accommodate a total of 150 students, servers, and teachers,
which would be done in future development phases as
donations become available.

Next steps

Ongoing research of the property will determine whether we can get the appropriate permits to develop a center for 150 people.
